Evergreen Kart

Something about user

Exactly what the name says, Evergreen kart is an online vegetable delivery app developed by Nijomee Technologies. To make it the best fresh vegetable delivery app we have built it on Native Android mobile application development technology. Evergreen kart is a Highly optimized online vegetable selling app in Rohtak where users can sell & purchase veggies like Broccoli, Cucumber, Cabbage, Tomato, Potato, Peas, and many more. Evergreen Kart is one of the most scalable android mobile application which facilitates fresh vegetables supply chain industry to digitally transform their traditional business to online mobile app or web app platforms.

Evergreen Kart is a B2B vegetable selling application where users can add products to the cart, place orders, track delivery status, and pay online.

This application is available in multiple languages to ease the life of common users. A complete app for vegetable delivery, Evergreen kart is a new market beast for selling vegetables online and target market business growth digitally.
